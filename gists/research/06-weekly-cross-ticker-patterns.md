# Weekly findings scan themes across tickers

Daily reports cover one ticker. **Weekly agents** scan the ledger for cross-ticker patterns.

Roles in the pipeline:

```
research (daily) → verify → consensus → patterns-weekly (~7d) → findings-weekly
```

`patterns-weekly` looks for recurring themes — capex anxiety, rate sensitivity, sector rotations.

`findings-weekly` flags breaking catalysts: regulatory actions, FDA decisions, macro shocks.

This is where crowd research becomes **market memory**, not isolated chat logs.
